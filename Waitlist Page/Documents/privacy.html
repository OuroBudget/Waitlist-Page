Privacy Notice

Waitlist · Last updated June 2026

Thanks for joining the OuroBudget waitlist. We like to keep things simple and transparent, so here's exactly what's going on with your information.

The short version: we collect your email, only to tell you about OuroBudget, and we never sell it or hand it to anyone.

What we collect

Just your email address. That's it. We don't ask for your name, your financial info, or anything else to join the waitlist.

Why we collect it

So we can let you know when OuroBudget launches, share occasional updates about our progress, and send you early-access details when they're ready. Nothing more.

Where it's stored

Your email is stored with Brevo, the email service we use to manage our waitlist and send updates. You can read about how Brevo handles data on their website. We can access this list to send you those updates.

Who we share it with

Nobody. We don't sell your email, rent it, or hand it to advertisers or third parties. Ever. The only company that touches it is Brevo, and only so we can email you.

How long we keep it

Until you ask us to remove it, or until OuroBudget launches and the waitlist has served its purpose. You can ask to be removed at any time.

Your choices

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link, and clicking it takes you off the list. You can also email us at privacy@ourobudget.app to be removed or to ask what we have on file.

Questions

Reach out anytime at privacy@ourobudget.app.
